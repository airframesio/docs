# Team

**Airframes** is built and maintained by a small team of aviation and radio enthusiasts.

## Core

- **Kevin Elliott** — Founder and lead developer. Kevin started Airframes to build a community-driven ACARS aggregation platform.

## Community

Airframes wouldn't exist without its community of feeders, contributors, and supporters. See the [Acknowledgments](/docs/acknowledgments) page for a list of individuals and projects that have helped shape Airframes.

## Get Involved

Interested in contributing? See [Contributing](/docs/contributing) for the many ways you can help — from feeding data to writing code to improving documentation.
