# Pricing

:::caution Under Development
Pricing tiers are being finalized. The information below reflects the current plan and may change before general availability.
:::

## Feeder Access (Free)

Active feeders who consistently contribute data to Airframes receive free API access. This is our way of giving back to the people who make Airframes possible.

**Requirements:**
- An active feeder station on Airframes
- Consistent feeding over at least 7 days

**Includes:**
- 60 requests per minute
- Access to messages, flights, aircraft, and station endpoints
- Personal and non-commercial use

## Commercial Access

Paid tiers for commercial users, researchers, and organizations with higher-volume needs are in development. Pricing will be structured to keep the service sustainable while remaining accessible.

If you have a specific use case or need early access, please [contact us](./contact.md) — we're happy to discuss options.
