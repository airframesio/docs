# Data Licensing & Attribution

Airframes aggregates aviation data contributed in real time by a **global
network of volunteer feeder stations**. When you use the API, you're building on
that community's effort — please use the data responsibly and credit the source.

## Attribution

If you display or redistribute Airframes data, please attribute it to
**Airframes** and the feeder community, for example:

> Data provided by [Airframes.io](https://airframes.io) and its community of feeders.

A link back to `https://airframes.io` is appreciated.

## Acceptable use

- The API is subject to the [Terms of Service](https://docs.airframes.io/api/terms).
- Respect the rate limits for your access tier (see [Pricing](./pricing.md)) —
  the `X-RateLimit-*` headers tell you where you stand.
- Don't attempt to re-identify or de-anonymize individuals from station or
  message data.

## Redistribution & commercial use

Bulk redistribution, resale, or commercial use beyond your access tier requires
prior arrangement. Contact [api@airframes.io](mailto:api@airframes.io) to discuss
licensing for your use case.

:::note Under development
The Airframes API and its licensing terms are still being finalized. This page
describes the intended approach; reach out at
[api@airframes.io](mailto:api@airframes.io) with any questions.
:::
